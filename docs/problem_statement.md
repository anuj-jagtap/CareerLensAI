# Problem Statement

## Project Name

CareerLens AI: AI-Powered Career Intelligence & Job Match Platform

## Background

Many freshers apply for hundreds of jobs but receive very few interview calls. Most candidates are unaware of:

* Which job roles match their profile?
* Which skills are currently demanded by employers?
* What skills they are missing?
* Why their resumes are not getting shortlisted?
* What they should learn next?

At the same time, thousands of IT job postings are published every day containing valuable information about required skills, technologies, experience levels, and market trends.

## Problem

There is a gap between fresher profiles and industry requirements. Freshers often make career decisions without data-driven insights.

## Proposed Solution

Develop an AI-powered platform that:

1. Analyzes IT job market trends
2. Extracts skills from job descriptions
3. Parses user resumes
4. Recommends suitable job roles
5. Calculates job-role match scores
6. Identifies skill gaps
7. Generates personalized career improvement roadmaps

## Target Users

* Freshers
* Final-year students
* Career switchers
* Entry-level job seekers

## Expected Impact

The platform will help users understand where they stand in the job market and how to improve their chances of getting shortlisted.
